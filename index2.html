About Powergym

Established in 2010, Powergym in New Ranip, Ahmedabad, has been a cornerstone of the local fitness community. We believe that fitness is a journey, not a destination. Our mission is to provide a supportive, motivating, and clean environment with top-of-the-line equipment and expert trainers to guide you every step of the way. Customer satisfaction is our top priority.
