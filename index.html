About Power Gym

Established in 2010, Power Gym has been a cornerstone of the New Ranip fitness community for over a decade.[1] We believe that a great workout starts with a great environment. Our gym is known for its vibrant, motivating, and empowering atmosphere where every member feels supported.[1, 2] We are not just a gym; we are a community passionate about achieving fitness goals together.

Our facility is meticulously maintained and equipped with top-notch, quality equipment to ensure you have the best tools for your fitness journey.[3] We pride ourselves on our cleanliness and hygiene, including sanitized restrooms, to provide a safe and comfortable space for everyone.[1]
